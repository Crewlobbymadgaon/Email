04.12 02:03 NIC Email ID Activation Notice

NIC Email Activation Instructions

Dear All,

The creation of email IDs in the new NIC mailing system for the users listed in the attached sheet has been successfully completed. Your proposed mail ID in the attached list is your new mail ID.

You have now to start using the new mailing system. Kindly follow the steps below:

  • Go to your browser and enter https://mail.gov.in
  • In the NIC eMail Services log-in page, enter your Email address and select Next.
  • Enter your existing email account password.
  • Click Sign In.
  • You will receive an OTP via SMS to your registered mobile number.
  • Enter the OTP and click Next.
  • You will be asked to reset your password. This new password will be used for all future logins.

Once completed, begin using the new mailing system. Sending mails through the old system may not be delivered, so it is mandatory to use the new one.

Group alias functionality will be rolled out soon. Until then, send emails to individual email IDs. The old system remains available in read-only mode for a short time.
